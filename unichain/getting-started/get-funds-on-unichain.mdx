## Bridge Funds With Uniswap
The easiest way to get funds on Unichain is to bridge them from Ethereum Mainnet on the Uniswap Interface. Simply connect your wallet select ETH as input token on Ethereum Mainnet and ETH as output token on Unichain.

Check it out on the [Uniswap Interface](https://app.uniswap.org/swap?chain=mainnet&outputChain=unichain&inputCurrency=NATIVE&outputCurrency=NATIVE).

## Bridge with a Partner Interface
Many of Unichain's ecosystem partners offer bridges to Unichain. Check out our [bridging page](https://www.unichain.org/bridge) page for details.

## Direct to Canonical Bridge
You can also quickly fund a Unichain Wallet directly with Unichain ETH or Unichain Sepolia ETH by ETH directly to the Unichain bridge. To do this:

| | Instruction | Mainnet | Sepolia |
|---| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Make sure your wallet is set to the correct network | Chain ID 1 | Chain ID 11155111  |
| 2 | Send ETH to the bridge contract | [0x81014F44b0a345033bB2b3B21C7a1A308B35fEeA](https://etherscan.io/address/0x81014F44b0a345033bB2b3B21C7a1A308B35fEeA) | [0xea58fcA6849d79EAd1f26608855c2D6407d54Ce2](https://sepolia.etherscan.io/address/0xea58fcA6849d79EAd1f26608855c2D6407d54Ce2) |
| 3 | Monitor the transaction in your block explorer of choice | [https://etherscan.io](https://sepolia.etherscan.io/) | [https://sepolia.etherscan.io](https://sepolia.etherscan.io/) |
| 4 | Switch your wallet to Unichain. Your ETH should be available there in a few minutes | Chain ID 130 (Unichain Mainnet) | Chain ID 1301 (Unichain Sepolia) |

## Use a Faucet with Unichain Sepolia
For development and testing, use one of our providers faucets to get testnet funds directly on Unichain Sepolia. See our [faucets page](/docs/unichain/tools/faucets) for more details.

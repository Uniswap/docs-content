## Alchemy

[Account Kit](https://accountkit.alchemy.com/) is the complete developer toolkit to build zero-friction user flows, from sign-up to checkout, in minutes. It includes:
- Embedded Wallets: onboard users with plug-and-play UI components, familiar social logins, and customizable branding.
- Smart Accounts: secure, gas-optimized, modular ERC-4337 smart contract accounts.
- Account Abstraction Infra: sponsor gas and transact with one-click using smart accounts.

Try our [demo](https://demo.alchemy.com/) and [start building](https://dashboard.alchemy.com/accounts?a=account-kit)!

- Unichain Sepolia Testnet

## Circle

[Circle's Programmable Wallets](https://www.circle.com/programmable-wallets) and Gas Station provide developer tools for integrating USDC and digital assets into web and mobile applications with gasless transactions.

* [**Programmable Wallets**](https://www.circle.com/programmable-wallets) allow developers to create and manage **Externally Owned Addresses (EOAs) and Smart Contract Accounts** with security backed by **multi-party computation (MPC) technology**. With intuitive SDKs and APIs, developers can integrate secure, user-friendly wallets directly into their apps.
* [**Gas Station**](https://developers.circle.com/w3s/gas-station) eliminates the complexity of gas fees by enabling sponsored transactions, allowing users to interact with blockchain applications without worrying about transaction costs.

Get started with Circle Programmable Wallets [here](https://hubs.li/Q034m_cc0).

## Privy

[Privy](http://privy.io/) is the easiest way for crypto app developers to onboard all users, supporting many of the most popular crypto apps. Privy offers [embedded wallets](https://www.privy.io/features/wallets) so you can seamlessly provision self-custodial wallets for users who sign in with email or social login, as well as [powerful connectors](https://www.privy.io/features/connectors) for crypto natives who prefer to sign in with their existing wallets. It's one library to onboard all users, regardless of where they are on their crypto journey. You can get started with Privy [here](http://dashboard.privy.io/)!

## ZeroDev

[ZeroDev](https://docs.zerodev.app/) is a chain-abstracted smart account that helps you build Web3 experiences that are:

- Free of gas: sponsor gas for your users or let them pay gas with an ERC20 token
- Free of approvals: safely skip confirmations through transaction automation
- Free of bridges: users can make cross-chain transactions without interacting with bridges
- Can easily move funds between CEXs and the account

Get started with ZeroDev [here](https://docs.zerodev.app/)!

## Superchain

The [Superchain Faucet](https://app.optimism.io/faucet) provides testnet ETH for Unichain. Anyone can claim 0.05 test ETH on 1 network every 24 hours, or verify your onchain identity for more tokens.

### Supported Networks

- [Unichain Sepolia Testnet](https://app.optimism.io/faucet)

## Circle

[Circle](https://faucet.circle.com/) offers an easy to use Unichain testnet faucet. You can use this faucet to claim Unichain Sepolia testnet USDC for free - one drip per network every hour.

### Supported Networks

- [Unichain Sepolia Testnet](https://faucet.circle.com/)

## QuickNode

[QuickNode](https://faucet.quicknode.com/unichain/sepolia) Faucet offers an easy to use Multi-Chain Faucet that supports Unichain testnet. You can use QuickNode Faucet to claim Unichain Sepolia testnet ETH for free - one drip per network every 12 hours.

### Supported Networks

- [Unichain Sepolia Testnet](https://faucet.quicknode.com/unichain/sepolia)

## thirdweb

[thirdweb](https://thirdweb.com/unichain-sepolia-testnet) faucet offers an easy to use Unichain testnet. You can use this Faucet to claim Unichain Sepolia testnet ETH for free - one drip per network every 24 hours.

### Supported Networks

- [Unichain Sepolia Testnet](https://thirdweb.com/unichain-sepolia-testnet)

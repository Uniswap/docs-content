## Superchain

The [Superchain Faucet](https://app.optimism.io/faucet) provides testnet ETH for Unichain. Anyone can claim 0.05 test ETH on one network every 24 hours, or verify onchain identity for more tokens.

### Supported networks

- [Unichain Sepolia Testnet](https://app.optimism.io/faucet)

## Circle

[Circle](https://faucet.circle.com/) offers an easy-to-use Unichain testnet faucet. You can use it to claim Unichain Sepolia testnet USDC for free (one drip per network every hour).

### Supported networks

- [Unichain Sepolia Testnet](https://faucet.circle.com/)

## QuickNode

[QuickNode Faucet](https://faucet.quicknode.com/unichain/sepolia) offers an easy-to-use multi-chain faucet that supports Unichain testnet. You can use it to claim Unichain Sepolia testnet ETH for free (one drip per network every 12 hours).

### Supported networks

- [Unichain Sepolia Testnet](https://faucet.quicknode.com/unichain/sepolia)

## Thirdweb

[thirdweb](https://thirdweb.com/unichain-sepolia-testnet) offers an easy-to-use Unichain testnet faucet. You can use it to claim Unichain Sepolia testnet ETH for free (one drip per network every 24 hours).

### Supported networks

- [Unichain Sepolia Testnet](https://thirdweb.com/unichain-sepolia-testnet)

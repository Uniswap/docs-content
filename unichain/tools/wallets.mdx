## Fireblocks

[Fireblocks](https://www.fireblocks.com/) is an easy-to-use platform to create new blockchain based products, and manage day-to-day digital asset operations. Exchanges, banks, PSPs, lending desks, custodians, trading desks, and hedge funds can securely scale their digital asset operations through the Fireblocks Network and MPC-based Wallet Infrastructure.

Get started with Fireblocks [here](https://www.fireblocks.com/).

### Supported networks
- Unichain Mainnet
- Unichain Sepolia Testnet

## Rainbow

[Rainbow](https://rainbow.me) is a market leading self-custodial Ethereum wallet that empowers users to securely manage their digital assets. Rainbow is pioneering consumer experiences in Web3 and is an active open-source member of the Ethereum community.

Get started with the RainbowKit [here](https://www.rainbowkit.com/docs/introduction).

### Supported networks
- Unichain Mainnet
- Unichain Sepolia Testnet

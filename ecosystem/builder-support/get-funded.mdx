[The Uniswap Foundation](https://www.uniswapfoundation.org/) and ecosystem contributors provide funding pathways for research, tooling, and product development.

## Before You Apply

1. **Deploy to Unichain and/or Uniswap v4.** Some programs consider early prototypes, but a working deployment can strengthen your application.
2. **Provide documentation.** Include clear documentation with setup instructions, project goals, and demo materials.
3. **Highlight your impact.** Measure and include metrics like user adoption, transaction volume, or community engagement.

## Uniswap Foundation Grants

The Uniswap Foundation funds projects across protocols, tooling, research, governance, and community initiatives.

**What you get**
- Funding based on project scope and needs
- Technical resources and ecosystem connections
- Strategic support: partnerships, distribution, and guidance

[Apply for Uniswap Foundation Grants](https://share.hsforms.com/1fxQjPQTgTYmPwlYxxKlSGQsdca9)

## Unichain Grants

Unichain Grants support teams building applications, infrastructure, and ecosystem tooling on Unichain.

**What you get**

- Funding support aligned to project scope and milestones
- Ecosystem support for teams building on Unichain
- Access to technical and builder resources

[Apply for Unichain Grants](https://share.hsforms.com/18Kv3hTvDSt-x1wK9va0OYwsdca9)

## Uniswap Hook Incubator

The Uniswap Hook Incubator is an async program focused on Uniswap v4 hook development.

**Who it is for**

- Experienced Solidity developers
- Teams with DeFi experience
- Builders exploring new hook-based primitives

**What you get**

- Fully covered tuition
- Up to $40k in prizes
- Access to DeFi experts and a Uniswap v4 builder community

[Apply for the Uniswap Hook Incubator](https://atrium.academy/uniswap)

## Uniswap v4 Hooks Support

This program provides support for building and shipping Uniswap v4 hooks.

[Apply for Uniswap v4 Hooks Support](https://share.hsforms.com/1O91L2eugREerAJJQt1jFTQs8pgg)

## UFSF Audit Subsidies

The Uniswap Foundation Security Fund (UFSF) provides access to security audits at reduced or no cost through a vetted provider process.

**What you get**

- Access to audit providers in the UFSF program
- Standardized deliverables and quality assurance
- For subsidy recipients: up to 100% of audit costs

[Apply for UFSF audit subsidies](https://areta.fillout.com/ufsf-projects)

---
title: 'Permissionless Fault Proofs Update'
description: 'Posted: December 18, 2024 | Effective: January 6, 2025'
---

On Monday, January 6, 2025, Unichain Sepolia underwent a scheduled upgrade to enable [permissionless fault proofs](https://specs.optimism.io/fault-proof/index.html). This milestone supported the broader rollout of Unichain as an open, trust-minimized system.

<Callout title="Action Required: Re-Prove Pending Withdrawals!" type="warn">
We recommended not starting any new withdrawals within 7 days of the upgrade, as withdrawals still pending on the day of the upgrade needed to be restarted.

For any still pending on January 6:

- These withdrawals no longer processed automatically.
- After confirmation that the upgrade completed, users needed to re-prove these withdrawals.
</Callout>

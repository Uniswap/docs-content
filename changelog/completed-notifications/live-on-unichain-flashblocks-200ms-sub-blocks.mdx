title: 'Live on Unichain: Flashblocks 200ms Sub-Blocks'
description: 'Posted: August 14, 2025 | Effective: August 14, 2025'
---

Unichain now confirms transactions every 200 milliseconds, making it one of the fastest chains available.

This makes Unichain the first chain to pair sub-second latency with provable ordering, delivering the speed users expect and integrity developers can build on.

<Callout type="info">
Flashblocks on Unichain were designed by Uniswap Labs and Flashbots to run inside Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs).
</Callout>

</Callout>

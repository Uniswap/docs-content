---
title: 'Live on Unichain: Fair Transaction Ordering and MEV Protection'
description: 'Posted: May 1, 2025 | Effective: May 1, 2025'
---

Unichain became the first L2 to build blocks inside a Trusted Execution Environment (TEE). This upgrade increased transparency in block building, reinforced priority ordering, mitigated extractive MEV, and introduced support for revert protection.

<Callout type="info">
Powered by [Rollup-Boost](https://writings.flashbots.net/introducing-rollup-boost), the TEE-based block builder on Unichain was built by Uniswap Labs and Flashbots.
</Callout>

Learn more in the [blog post](https://blog.uniswap.org/rollup-boost-is-live-on-unichain) and [whitepaper](https://docs.unichain.org/whitepaper.pdf).
</Callout>

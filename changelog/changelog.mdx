## New Feature Policies

Uniswap Labs is committed to continuous improvement across the Uniswap stack, including APIs, protocols, SDKs, documentation, and related developer tooling. From time to time, we may offer new features, some of which may be backwards compatible and some of which may require new integration. When we have something new to share, we will post it here and reflect it across the relevant documentation. Once a new feature has reached maturity, we may remove the notice, but you can continue to find the supported behavior in our docs and guides.

## Sunsetting Policies

To continue offering state-of-the-art developer tooling and infrastructure, Uniswap Labs may deprecate unused or outdated features over time. Sunsetting allows teams to reduce technical debt, simplify ongoing maintenance, and focus support on workflows that align with current product direction and user needs.

When Uniswap Labs determines that a feature or service will be sunset, a notice will be posted here. We will ensure that notice is posted at least 30 days ahead of scheduled non-backwards compatible changes and will endeavor to post notices at least 60 days in advance where possible. Where applicable, we will also indicate that a feature is deprecated in relevant documentation and guides.

Not sooner than 1 week after the change has occurred, we will take down the notice and remove the outdated feature details from the relevant documentation and guides.
